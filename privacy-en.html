Privacy Policy – MID App

Last updated: 3 October 2025

MID Consultings ("we", "our", or "us") operates the MID App ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Account information: email address (required for login).
  • Optional information: country, name, city, bio, profile picture, and any other content you choose to share in your profile.
  • Usage data: interactions with features (e.g., mood tracking, test responses).
  • Payment data: we do not collect payment details directly – all purchases are processed by the app stores.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected data to:

  • Provide and improve app features.
  • Personalize user experience and analytics.
  • Respond to support requests.
  • Ensure security and prevent abuse.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. Data may be shared with trusted service providers (e.g., hosting, analytics) who process data on our behalf. Information may be disclosed if required by law.

4. Data Retention

Profile data is kept as long as your account is active. Deleting your account removes your profile and related data from our systems.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your data within the app.
  • Request deletion of your account at any time.
  • Contact us for any privacy-related concerns.
